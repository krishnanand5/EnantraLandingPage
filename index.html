After a Successful Version 1.0 Last Year, We are gearing up for 2.0.

VERSION 1.0

Enantra envisions to boost the spirit of Entrepreneurship and provide a platform for budding ideas. The main vision of Enantra is to provide a proper connect between the corporate, professional and entrepreneurship world inside the campus. It helps in providing the right platform for students with budding talents and helps in escalating their ideas. It includes several workshops, events, talk shows which are quite engaging and exhilarating. The three day fest will involve many known personalities and start-up enthusiasts from all around the city. This year we are entering into another venture aiming to make the next edition a bigger success.

Flagship Events

RUNWAY

It is an on-spot Start-up Investment Platform where aspiring entrepreneur-contestants make business presentations to a panel of investors, who then decide whether or not to invest in that particular start-up. Start-ups were registered from all across the country and top 8 were chosen for live pitching on 24th and 25th February, at CEG. The start-ups failed to receive investment but got critically acclaimed by Unitus Seed Funds, CII, TiE Chennai and Chennai Angels.

6 DEGREE TALKS

A professional interactive talk session, which is interactive in both physical and virtual level, featuring motivational speakers, Entrepreneurs and Corporates. It was held every evening from 23rd to 25th of February. The speakers were Mr. Nagraj of FSS, Mr. Karthik Kumar of Evam, Mr. Kiruba Shankar, Mr. Naveen Valsakumar, Mr. Kavipriya Anandan, Hip Hop Tamizha Adhi, Auto Annadurai, Mr. Vikas Malpani and Mr. Varun Agarwal.

GOOGLE STARTUP WEEKEND

54 hour Hackathon was supported and organized by ‘Google for Entrepreneurs’. It was held between the 24th and 26th of February. It was named CEG Startup Weekend to brand CEG.

BRIDGE

A Start-up Expo with Job and Internship Fair. About 20 startups were presented in Hall number – 13 to provide Job and Internship along with branding. Students all across the state got an opportunity to interact and around 18 students received internship offers and 1 student received a Job Offer.

THE COMMUNITY

Capitalize is a student entrepreneurship club, founded mainly to instigate young pioneers and start-up enthusiasts. Capitalize is an essential student limb of the CED chapter in Chennai. This club incubates blooming ideas – no matter how eccentric or miniscule. Equipped with a core team of courageous creative minds, Capitalize challenges, tests and motivates its members to follow their goals and prove their success. The events held by the club, helps to instil start-up culture amongst participants and also reflects its drive for innovation, creativity and leadership.
